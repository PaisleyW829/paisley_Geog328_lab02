Bilewater

This is a map from HollowKnight: Silk Song.


Bilewater consists of damp, root-filled tunnels covered in thick thorns. The caves and pools here are contaminated by toxic runoff that seeps down from the Citadel above. At the far eastern edge of Bilewater, beneath the Pale Lake, lies the only place where the water remains pure and untouched by the pollution.
