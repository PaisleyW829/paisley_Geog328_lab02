Sub Areas

Exhaust Organ

The Exhaust Organ is a vast mechanical structure beneath the Underworks, linking it to Bilewater. Reaching it requires passing through The Mist. When the Haunting began, Grand Mother Silk sent Phantom to guard the Exhaust Organ and stop any bugs traveling from Sinner’s Road from reaching the Citadel.

Bilehaven

Bilehaven sits at the upper edge of Bilewater, just beneath the Putrified Ducts. It serves as a village for the Stilkin and is home to their leader, Groal the Great.

Weavenest Murglin

Weavenest Murglin lies in the far southeastern part of Bilewater. It’s accessed by a Needolin door after swimming through a tunnel swarming with Muckmaggots.
